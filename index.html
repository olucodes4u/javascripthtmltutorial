A little Introduction About SoLife

Society for Life Changers and Good Parental Care {SoLife} is a non-governmental, not-for profit organization and women led organization working on human right, health, children, women and youth development, registered with Corporate Affair Commission (CAC) in Nigeria, The organization started working in 2009 and received CAC certificate 2015. Our organization was established as a respond to the community needs for the control and prevention of diseases, human right issues, adolescent sexual and reproductive, child morbidity and mortality, women and girl economic empowerment and women participation in governance. The success of the organization has been on the increase and depended on emerging needs of the communities where it works. Its activities are not only community driven but community ownership is weaved into the design of every community project from the outset; hence sustainability is ensured. Critical aspect of our strategic vision is that, every programme has social inclusion of women and girl groups and the incorporation of persons from these groups in public offices. read more...
